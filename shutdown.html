Allied EMS & RN Prep

Official Notice of Service Suspension

An Important Announcement Regarding Our Services

It is with regret that we announce the immediate cessation of services for Allied EMS & RN Prep, effective today. We want to extend our deepest gratitude to all the students, instructors, and partners who have been a part of our journey in advancing pre-hospital and nursing education.

Reason for Closure:

This difficult decision was made following an extensive financial review, primarily due to increasing operational costs and sustained low website traffic. Regrettably, the current resources are no longer sufficient to maintain the quality and scope of services you expect and deserve.

Permanent Site Disablement:

Please note that the Allied EMS & RN Prep website address, alliedemsrnprep.com (or similar), will be permanently retired and disabled in August of 2026. This includes all associated content, databases, and login portals.

We encourage any remaining users to secure any necessary documentation or records before this date.

Thank you for your understanding and support over the program We wish you the very best in your professional careers.
