About Allied

Our Mission: Free Education

Allied was created and is currently operated by a current nursing student who understands the financial burden of pursuing a career in healthcare. Our mission is built on the frustration of constantly paying for study guides and prep courses.

We are committed to eliminating these financial barriers, providing 100% free, high-quality study resources for both Nursing and EMS students, fostering a community of peer support and accessible education.

Why Choose Allied?

  • Completely Free: All resources are provided at absolutely no cost, ensuring equity in education.
  • Peer-Created Content: Materials developed and personally curated by a student, for students, ensuring content is highly relevant to current academic needs.
  • No Expert Talk: The content is driven by focused, real-world study experience, not external claims of expertise.

Disclaimer: Allied Prep resources are not affiliated with or endorsed by any certifying body or licensing board. Resources are provided for free as supplemental study tools only.
