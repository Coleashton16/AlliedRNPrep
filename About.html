Our Story

Born from a student's perspective, AlliedRNPrep is dedicated to making the path through nursing school clearer and more manageable for everyone.

Our Mission

AlliedRNPrep was founded on a simple yet powerful idea: to provide nursing students with the supplementary resources they actually need, curated from the perspective of someone who has walked the same path. We understand the challenges, the late-night study sessions, and the complexities of the curriculum.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between textbook knowledge and practical application, offering clear, concise, and relevant study aids that support your core education and build your confidence for exams, clinicals, and the NCLEX.

Meet the Founder

My name is Cole Ashton, and I am the founder of AlliedRNPrep. As a dedicated nursing student, I experienced firsthand the challenges of balancing rigorous coursework with the financial strain of accessing quality study resources. Frustrated by the high costs and limited availability of comprehensive study tools, I was motivated to create a solution that would benefit myself and fellow nursing students.

With a passion for education and a commitment to student success, I developed AlliedRNPrep to provide high-quality, targeted supplemental study materials tailored specifically for nursing students. My goal is to bridge the gap between expensive, often inaccessible resources and the need for reliable, effective study aids that promote mastery of essential nursing concepts.

At AlliedRNPrep, I strive to deliver content that is accurate, practical, and easy to understand, helping students to confidently prepare for exams and clinical assessments. I believe that every nursing student deserves access to affordable and comprehensive resources to support their academic journey and future professional endeavors.

Thank you for visiting AlliedRNPrep. I am dedicated to supporting your success and look forward to being a part of your path toward becoming a skilled and compassionate nurse.

- Cole Ashton, Founder of AlliedRNPrep
